Active Membership

Would you like to be an Active Member of Women in Computing? As an active member, you will have access to memers-only perks such as networking events, resume review, conferences, tours, etc. Your resume will be forwarded to our partners and will be eligible to apply for WiC-affiliated Scholarships to attend conferences. Active members will also be eligible to run for office upon completion of internship program.

Become an Active Member

Requirements

  • Have a positive outlook and be supportive of each other.
  • Represent WiC values in-person and online.
  • Actively attend WiC events or programs throughout the semester.
  • Membership Fee of $10, renewed annually.
Join Mailing List

Once you have joined our mailing list, please attend our next meeting or event and notify us that you would like to be active member. Please have your membership dues ready. If you are unable to attend, please send us an email at csulbwic@gmail.com stating your interest on being an active member. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to us!
